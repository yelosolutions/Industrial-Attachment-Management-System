How to apply for an Attachment

  • Go to the application page and fill in the request form.
  • Wait for application approval from COD.
  • Attachment letter will then be generated by COD.
  • Download attachment letter.
  • Wait for finance clearance of the application request.
  • Wait for liason office generation of logbook.
  • Download generated logbook.
  • Update details of place of attachment.
  • Upload signed logbook.
  • Wait for lectures remark and score.
